What we actually built

The idea started simple: job seekers are getting filtered out before a human ever sees their name. Resumes rejected by algorithms. Interview nerves killing candidates who are genuinely qualified. Career coaches charging $200 an hour for something that should be accessible to everyone.

So we built two tools.

🎙 PREPT AI Live
Real-time interview coach. Listens during your Zoom call and delivers coached answers in under 3 seconds. Works on your phone , invisible to screen sharing.
📄 PREPT AI Match
ATS resume optimizer. Scores your resume against any job description, fills in missing keywords, rewrites your summary, and exports the optimized version.
💰 Salary Negotiation
Enter your offer. Get a specific counter-offer range, a word-for-word script, and three power phrases backed by real compensation data.
🤝 Duo Mode
Invite a coach, mentor, or family member to see your questions in real time and send notes to your screen. Human plus AI support simultaneously.

That is not counting filler word detection, progress tracking, the one-way video coach, cover letter generator, LinkedIn optimizer, or application tracker. It is a full career suite at $12 a month. Final Round AI charges $149 for less.

What I believe about this product

The job market in 2026 is not a level playing field. It never was. Candidates with connections get referrals. Candidates with money get coaches. Candidates who know how to game the ATS get callbacks. Everyone else sends 40 applications and hears nothing.

PREPT AI exists to close that gap. Not to help people fake qualifications they do not have , to help people articulate the qualifications they already have, clearly and confidently, in the 6 seconds a recruiter spends on a resume and the 30 minutes that determine whether they get an offer.

The product is not perfect. There are features on the roadmap that are not built yet. There are edge cases I have not found. The Trustpilot page has no reviews because we launched today.

But the core works. The live coach delivers real answers in under 3 seconds. The resume optimizer finds real keyword gaps. The salary negotiation coach gives real counter-offer ranges. I have tested all of it personally, repeatedly, at 3am, fixing things until they were right.
