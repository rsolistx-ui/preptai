About PREPT AI

Built out of frustration.
Launched out of necessity.

The job market is broken in ways most people don't realize until they're in the middle of it — sending application after application into silence.

The problem we set out to solve

It started the same way it starts for most people — applying for positions that were a clear fit, then hearing nothing. Not a rejection. Not feedback. Just silence.

The reason, it turns out, has nothing to do with qualifications. 75% of resumes are rejected by automated software before a human ever reads them. Applicant Tracking Systems — the algorithms companies use to filter candidates — discard resumes that don't contain the exact keywords they're programmed to look for. A resume that took hours to write can be eliminated in milliseconds for missing a phrase like "CRM software" or "SLA compliance."

Then there's the interview itself. Even when you get the call, the pressure of a live Zoom interview — especially for people who haven't interviewed in years, or who are transitioning into a new field, or who simply communicate better when they have a moment to think — can make qualified candidates stumble on questions they know the answer to.

These are solvable problems. We built PREPT AI to solve them.

What PREPT AI does

PREPT AI Match analyzes your resume against any job description, identifies every keyword the ATS is scanning for, and rewrites your resume to pass the filter — while preserving your voice and your actual experience. It also generates a tailored cover letter for each job, optimizes your LinkedIn profile using the same keywords, and gives you salary intelligence so you walk into negotiations prepared.

PREPT AI Live sits alongside your Zoom or Teams call and listens to the interviewer's questions in real time. Within seconds it generates a confident, personalized answer based on your background and the specific role — so you always have something solid to work from, even for questions you didn't prepare for.

Together, they cover the entire pipeline: get past the algorithm to land the interview, then show up to the interview prepared to close.

Who we built this for

PREPT AI was built for anyone navigating a job search — whether you're a recent graduate applying for your first professional role, a seasoned professional who hasn't interviewed in a decade, someone making a career change into a new industry, or a skilled worker who knows their craft but struggles to translate that on paper.

We built it especially for people who are intelligent, capable, and qualified — but who the current hiring system was never designed to recognize.

Where we're based

PREPT AI is built and operated out of San Antonio, Texas. We're a small, focused team with one goal: make the job search more honest for people who deserve a fair shot.
