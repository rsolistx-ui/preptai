Founder Note
March 24, 2026 · Richard Solis

We're live. Here's what I want you to know.

This has been a passion project a long time in the making, and I'm genuinely excited to finally put it out there for people to use.

The idea behind PREPT AI came from a frustration I think a lot of people share — the experience of being qualified for a job, putting real effort into an application, and then hearing absolutely nothing back. No rejection, no feedback, just silence. After enough of that, you start to wonder if something is broken in the process. Turns out, something is.

Most people don't know that the majority of resumes are filtered out by software before a human ever reads them. These systems — called Applicant Tracking Systems — scan for specific keywords and discard anything that doesn't match, regardless of how strong the candidate actually is. It's not personal. It's algorithmic. And most job seekers are losing the game before they even know they're playing it.

That's what PREPT AI Match fixes. And then there's the interview side — which is its own challenge entirely. PREPT AI Live is the tool I wish I'd had sitting on my second screen every time I took a call unprepared.

I built this because I believe people deserve a fair shot. Not an unfair advantage — a fair shot. The tools to present themselves as well as they actually are.

I would genuinely love your feedback.

If something isn't working, tell me. If something surprised you in a good way, tell me that too. If there's a feature you wish existed, I want to hear it. You can reach me directly at richard@solisequityholdings.com — I read every message.

Thank you for being here at the beginning. This one's for you.
