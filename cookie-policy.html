Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how PREPT AI uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit preptai.co.

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit.

Cookies we use

Essential cookies

These are required for the service to function. They cannot be disabled.

supabase-auth-token
Keeps you logged in to your account. Expires when you log out or after 7 days of inactivity.
preptai-session
Stores your current session preferences such as selected sector and profile settings. Session cookie — deleted when you close your browser.

Analytics cookies

We use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how people use PREPT AI. No personally identifiable information is collected. No third-party advertising networks have access to this data.

Cookies we do NOT use

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting cookies, social media tracking pixels, or any cookies that track you across other websites.

Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from staying logged in. Most browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies — consult your browser's help documentation for instructions.
