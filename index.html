FAQ

Common questions.

Is this actually legal to use during an interview?
Yes. Using PREPT Live during a virtual interview is no different from having notes next to your monitor — which every interviewer knows candidates do. You are not fabricating credentials or deceiving anyone about your abilities. You are using a tool to articulate what you already know. Interviewers regularly use checklists and scripts during calls themselves.
Will the resume rewriter make my resume sound generic?
No — and this is specifically what we designed against. PREPT AI rewrites using your actual experience as the foundation. It inserts the keywords the ATS is scanning for, but threads them into sentences built around your real background, not templates. The summary and bullets still sound like you — just you with the right vocabulary for the job.
How does PREPT AI actually work during a Zoom call?
You open PREPT AI on a second monitor, a tablet, or your phone — somewhere the interviewer's camera cannot see it. The mic button listens to the interviewer's question (or you can type it manually), and within 2-3 seconds you get a coached answer tailored to your profile, sector, and the specific company you're interviewing at. You glance at it and speak naturally. No one can tell.
Is my resume data stored or shared?
No. Your resume content and job description are processed in real time to generate your analysis and rewrites. They are not stored on our servers, sold, or used to train AI models. The analysis exists only for your session.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account settings and your access continues until the end of your billing period. We want you to stay because PREPT AI works, not because canceling is difficult.
What sectors does PREPT Live cover?
14 sectors currently: General, Legal, Healthcare, Technology, Retail, Hospitality, Logistics, Finance, Education, Remote Work, Customer Service, Skilled Trades, Sales, and Admin/Office. Within each sector you can specify your exact job title for even more targeted coaching. More sectors are added regularly.
