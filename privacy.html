Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

PREPT AI ("we," "us," or "our") operates preptai.co. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information we collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google.

Resume and job description content: When you use PREPT AI Match, you submit resume text and job descriptions for analysis. This content is processed in real time to generate your results and is not stored on our servers after your session ends.

Usage data: We collect basic usage information such as which features you use and how often, to improve the product. This data is anonymized and never sold.

Payment information: Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

How we use your information

We use your information to: provide and improve the PREPT AI service; send transactional emails such as receipts and account notifications; respond to your support requests; and analyze usage patterns to improve the product.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your resume content to train AI models.

Data storage and security

Account data is stored securely using Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption. Resume and job description content submitted for analysis is processed in real time and discarded after your session — it is never written to a database.

We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. Payments are secured by Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure.

Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Your rights

Children's privacy

PREPT AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy
